WHAT IS FLI?

Family Leadership Initiative (FLI) is AIPAC’s brand new, exclusive, year-long training course for New York Metro area high school students and parents. The program is launching in January 2019 and is limited to 10 families.

Families will graduate with concrete tools, effective strategies, and key relationships to strengthen the U.S.-Israel relationship on your campus and in your community. Throughout the year, parents and students will attend interactive training workshops both together and separately. They will implement lessons learned by completing initiatives that tangibly impact U.S. support for Israel.

Over the course of a year, participants will learn the ins and outs of how AIPAC works and develop a deeper understanding of AIPAC’s strategy.

Perhaps most importantly, FLI will help families become effective pro-Israel activists together as a unit, empowering multiple generations with the knowledge and support system to make a difference.

FLI is a selective program requiring candidates to submit an application before being accepted.



Who should apply?

AIPAC club member families with a passion for Israel and America and a desire to be pro-Israel activists on campus and in the community. All levels of knowledge and experience are welcome.

*Application deadline is November 15, 2018. For more information, please contact Associate Director of Manhattan Communities AIPAC club member at any level (minimum giving $1,800) when starting the program


What are the commitments?

Once admitted, one student and at least one parent commit to attending the following:

January 13: Kickoff & Activism Training

February 7: Legislative & Policy Digital Briefing

March 24-26: AIPAC Policy Conference in Washington, DC with a special FLI reception

April TBD: On Campus: Real Life College Experience (students only)

June 2: Reflections & Presentations

September 15: Building Bridges With The Wider Community

November: AIPAC Schusterman Advocacy Institute High School Summit (students only)

November: Graduation & Tools For Future

Dates are subject to change.

*AIPAC Policy Conference is March 24-26 in Washington, D.C. Please note that participants are responsible for Policy Conference registration, travel and hotel arrangements. Registration information is at www.policyconference.org.

How will I know if I've been accepted?

Applications are due by Thursday, November 15th. Accepted participants will be notified by email the first week of December.
